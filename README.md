# Olist Strategic Data Intelligence: Logistics & Consumer Insights
**Student:** Amna | **ID:** GH1039590

## 1. Executive Summary
This project analyzes the Olist E-commerce ecosystem (2016-2018) to solve a critical "Logistics Crisis." By processing over 100,000 orders, the analysis identifies how delivery delays and high freight costs directly impact customer satisfaction (Review Scores).

## 2. Project Goals
- **Identify Geographic Gaps:** Mapping delivery times across Brazil's 27 states.
- **Seasonality Stress Test:** Analyzing the impact of Black Friday (November) on system latency.
- **Customer Trust:** Establishing the "Trust Threshold" where delivery delays lead to 1-star reviews.

## 3. Data Pipeline
- **Section 1:** Business Context & Audience Identification.
- **Section 2:** Quantitative Data Audit (Missing Values & Integrity).
- **Section 3:** Preprocessing (Feature Engineering & Cleaning).
- **Section 4:** 10 Strategic Visualisations & Actionable Recommendations.

## 4. Tech Stack
- **Tools:** Python, Google Colab, GitHub.
- **Libraries:** Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib, Plotly.
